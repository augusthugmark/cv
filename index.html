About Me

From a young age, I’ve been curious about the world around me. How people connect and how creativity brings clarity to complexity. Music taught me that even emotions can be translated into structure and form, with the added touch of the human intention. That same curiosity eventually led me to programming, already deeply motivated by the potential to solve real-world problems and create meaningful solutions through code. Years of making music have shaped how I approach creativity, collaboration, and continuous learning. These are skills I bring into the tech world. Whether I’m analyzing a piece of music or debugging code, I’m always focused on refining my craft. Outside of work, I remain curious about the world in both the big and small things. It might be the strategy of chess or the storytelling found in film. For me, life is about balancing logic and imagination and growing through both.
